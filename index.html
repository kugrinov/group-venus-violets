The Ultimate Guide to Greek Myth

Where it all began

Greek Mythology originated during the Bronze Age in Ancient Greece. The folklore informs us on how ancient groups of people viewed the world around them. The first ancient myths were first passed through oral tradition and eventually written texts were developed based on the mythology such as The Illiad and The Odyssey. The mythology boasts a wide pantheon of deities, classic heroic tales, and iconic mythical creatures. Many of the characters and myths from Greek Mythology are still known today and majorly influenced present-day stories.
